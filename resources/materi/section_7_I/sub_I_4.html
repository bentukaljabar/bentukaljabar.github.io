  1. Selesaikan operasi Pembagian pecahan bentuk aljabar \[ \left ( \frac{2x}{3y}+1\right ) \div \frac{x}{y} = . . . ?\]


Jawab :


\[=\]

. . .
. . .

Jawaban Salah

Pembahasan :

\[ = \left ( \frac{2x}{3y}+1\right ) \div \frac{x}{y}\]

\[= \left ( \frac{2x}{3y}+\frac{3y}{3y}\right ) \times \frac{y}{x}\]

\[= \left ( \frac{2x+3y}{3y}\right ) \times \frac{y}{x}\]

\[= \frac{(2x+3y) \times y }{3y \times x}\]

\[= \frac{2xy+3y^2}{3xy}\]

\[= \frac{2x+3y}{3x}\]
